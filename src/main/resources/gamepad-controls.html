Auto-Detection: Gamepads are automatically detected when connected. The game will switch to gamepad mode and show "Gamepad" in the HUD. Disconnect the gamepad to return to keyboard/mouse controls.

Movement & Aiming

Left Stick Move player (analog movement)
Right Stick Aim direction (relative to player position)
D-Pad Move player (digital movement, fallback)

Combat

Right Trigger (RT) Fire primary weapon
Left Trigger (LT) Fire secondary weapon
A Button Fire primary weapon (alternative)
B Button Fire secondary weapon (alternative)

Weapon Management

X Button Reload weapon
Y Button Switch to secondary weapon

Movement Modifiers

Left Stick Click (LS) Sprint
Right Bumper (RB) Sprint (alternative)
Left Bumper (LB) Jump/Space action

System Controls

Start Button Menu/Pause (if implemented)
Back/Select Button Scoreboard toggle (if implemented)

ðŸ’¡ Tips for Gamepad Play

  • Aiming: Use the right stick to aim in any direction relative to your player - much more intuitive than cursor movement!
  • Movement: Left stick provides smooth analog movement - push lightly for slow movement, fully for running
  • Firing: Triggers provide analog control - light press for single shots, full press for rapid fire
  • Switching: You can switch between gamepad and keyboard/mouse at any time
  • Deadzone: Small stick movements are ignored to prevent drift - adjust if needed
